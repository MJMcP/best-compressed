Daily tips


  • Change garments daily to ensure best fit for your compression needs.
  • Unless instructed otherwise, put on before you stand up from your bed in the morning.
  • Do not apply any oils, creams or lotions to the area of skin about to be covered with the garment, unless instructed by medical professionals.
  • Replace any daily used compression hoisery every 3-6 months to ensure best compression.
