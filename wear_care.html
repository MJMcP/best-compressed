Wear & Care


You're wearing your garment for a reason and the best way to ensure that you get the best results, for the longest time, is with proper aftercare.


Below are a small selection of videos and tips of how to wear, wash and ensure easy wearing for not only your garment but for your skin as well.



Daily tips


  • Change garments daily to ensure best fit for your compression needs.
  • Unless instructed otherwise, put on before you stand up from your bed in the morning.
  • Do not apply any oils, creams or lotions to the area of skin about to be immediately covered with the garment, unless instructed by medical professionals.
  • Replace any compression hoisery every 3-6 months to ensure best compression.
  • Avoid long hot baths or showers that may irritate the areas needing compression, causing them.
