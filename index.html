What is Compression?


Compression is a form of therapy used to help with the healing and eleviation of pain to injured areas of the body.


The area affected is covered with an elasticated material which is purposely designed to deliver a set type of pressure and support depending on the type of therapy provided.


It is important to recognise the differences in compression garments and the assistance they can provide to our general health and well being.

Wear & Care


The wearing and aftercare of compression garments is important to ensure that the best results are seen over the course of the therarpy.


Aftercare not only refers to the washing and upkeep of the garment but also to the skin that the garment is worn over.
