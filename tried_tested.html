Have You Any Tried and Tested Advice?


The best advice can often be the voice of experience. If you have any advice or quick tips and would like to contribute, please let us know in the text field provided.

If you are looking for information on any particular condition, here are a few organisations we would recommend.


National Blood Clot Alliance

www.stoptheclot.org

A non-profit, voluntary health organization dedicated to advancing the prevention, early diagnosis and successful treatment of life-threatening blood clots such as deep vein thrombosis, pulmonary embolism, and clot-provoked stroke.

Cancer Research UK Lymphoedema - Resources and Supports

www.cancerresearchuk.org

As one of the largest cancer research organisations outside of the USA, Cancer Research UK has a variety of resources available for those living with this side effect of cancer treatment.

World Health Organisation on Air Travel

www.who.int

The World Health Organisation's recommended advice for air travel with health concerns.
